What's Included in Standard Cleaning

Our standard house cleaning service covers all the essential areas of your home with meticulous attention to detail.

Kitchen

  • Clean and sanitize countertops and backsplash
  • Wipe down exterior of appliances
  • Clean sink and faucet
  • Sweep and mop floors
  • Empty trash and replace liner
  • Wipe down cabinet fronts

Bathrooms

  • Scrub and disinfect toilet, sink, and tub/shower
  • Clean mirrors and fixtures
  • Sweep and mop floors
  • Empty trash and replace liner
  • Wipe down surfaces and countertops
  • Clean glass shower doors

Bedrooms & Living Areas

  • Dust all surfaces and furniture
  • Vacuum carpets and rugs
  • Sweep and mop hard floors
  • Make beds (if linens are available)
  • Empty trash bins
  • Wipe down baseboards and door frames
