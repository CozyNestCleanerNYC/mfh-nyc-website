What's Included in Deep Cleaning

Our deep cleaning service includes everything in standard cleaning PLUS detailed attention to areas often overlooked in regular cleaning.

Kitchen Deep Clean

  • Clean inside refrigerator, oven, and microwave
  • Scrub and degrease range hood and filters
  • Wash cabinet fronts and handles thoroughly
  • Clean behind and under appliances
  • Scrub backsplash grout lines
  • Deep clean sink and remove mineral deposits
  • Sanitize trash cans inside and out

Bathroom Deep Clean

  • Remove scale from shower doors and fixtures
  • Scrub grout lines with specialized cleaners
  • Clean inside medicine cabinets and drawers
  • Remove soap scum and hard water stains
  • Clean exhaust fan and light fixtures
  • Sanitize and polish all chrome fixtures
  • Deep clean behind toilet and around base

Detailed Room Cleaning

  • Wipe down all baseboards and door frames
  • Clean window tracks and blinds
  • Dust ceiling fans and light fixtures
  • Remove dust from vents and air returns
  • Clean behind and under furniture
  • Vacuum under beds and heavy furniture
  • Polish wood surfaces and remove buildup

Time Investment

Deep cleaning takes 4-14+ hours depending on home size. We recommend scheduling deep cleaning before starting regular service or seasonally for best results.
