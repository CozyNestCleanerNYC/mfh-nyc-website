Professional Deep Cleaning Services in NYC

Our deep cleaning service goes far beyond regular house cleaning to provide a comprehensive, top-to-bottom transformation of your home. Perfect for spring cleaning, preparing for special events, moving into a new place, or when your home needs that extra level of attention and care.

Unlike our standard cleaning service, deep cleaning includes detailed work on areas that are typically cleaned less frequently. We clean inside appliances, scrub grout lines, wash baseboards, clean behind furniture, and tackle built-up grime and dirt that accumulates over time. This service is ideal for homes that haven't been professionally cleaned in a while or need a fresh start.

Our flat rate pricing means you know exactly what you'll pay upfront - no surprises, no hourly charges that can add up. We send 2 experienced cleaners for properties larger than 1 bedroom to ensure efficient, thorough service. Every deep clean includes our signature GoPro documentation so you can see the incredible transformation of your space.

As a family-run business serving NYC for years, we take pride in delivering exceptional results that exceed your expectations. Our team is fully insured and bonded, and we use only eco-friendly products that are safe for your family and pets while being tough on dirt and grime.
