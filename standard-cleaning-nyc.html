What's Included in Our Standard Cleaning Service

Kitchen Deep Clean

  • Clean and sanitize all countertops and surfaces
  • Wipe down exterior of all appliances (refrigerator, oven, microwave, dishwasher)
  • Clean stovetop, range hood, and backsplash
  • Scrub and sanitize sink and faucet
  • Clean cabinet fronts and handles
  • Empty trash and replace liner
  • Sweep and mop floors with appropriate cleaners

Bathroom Thorough Clean

  • Scrub and disinfect toilet inside and out
  • Clean shower/tub, including tiles and fixtures
  • Polish mirrors and glass surfaces
  • Sanitize sink, faucet, and countertops
  • Clean cabinet fronts and handles
  • Empty trash and replace liner
  • Sweep and mop floors with disinfectant

Living Areas & Bedrooms

  • Dust all surfaces including furniture, shelves, and decorative items
  • Vacuum all carpets and rugs thoroughly
  • Vacuum and mop hard floors
  • Clean mirrors and glass surfaces
  • Make beds and straighten pillows
  • Empty trash baskets and replace liners
  • Light dusting of baseboards and window sills
