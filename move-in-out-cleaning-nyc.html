Professional Move-In/Out Cleaning Services in NYC

Moving is stressful enough without worrying about getting your security deposit back or preparing your new home. Our specialized move-in/out cleaning service is designed specifically for empty homes and apartments, providing the thorough, detailed cleaning that landlords expect and new tenants deserve.

Our move-out cleaning service comes with a security deposit back guarantee. If your landlord finds any cleaning issues that prevent you from getting your full deposit back, we'll return to fix them at no additional charge. We understand NYC rental standards and know exactly what landlords look for during move-out inspections.

For move-in cleaning, we prepare your new home so it's spotless and ready for you to settle in. There's nothing better than moving into a space that's been professionally cleaned from top to bottom. We clean areas that are often overlooked, ensuring your new home is truly fresh and sanitized.

Our empty home specialization means we can work more efficiently without furniture and belongings in the way. We can access every corner, baseboard, and surface that's typically hidden. This allows us to provide a more thorough clean at a better value than traditional cleaning services.
